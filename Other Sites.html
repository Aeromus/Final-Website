Other Sites

Here is a list of some other sites that you might wanna check out to learn about encryption / crypto

Crypto And Privacy Village

These guys show up every year at the hacker convention Def-Con and have talks and demonstrations about Cryptography

Site: https://cryptovillage.org/

Learncryptography.com

A site thats sole goal is to teach people about cryptography and the why it is important

Site: https://learncryptography.com/

Electronic Frontier Foundation

The Electronic Frontier Foundation helps keep everyone informed about the current trending cyber security risks in the world. From government laws that threaten onine security to the latest data breech, they have it all and are here to protect your rights in the digital world.

Site: https://www.eff.org/

DEF-CON

DEF-CON is the worlds largest hacking convention. It hosts talks that range from computer newbie to l33t HaX0r. They have a wide range of events and villages that all aim to teach something new to attendies. Their website also has a media server that hosts all the previous years speakers and presntations.

Site: https://www.defcon.org/
